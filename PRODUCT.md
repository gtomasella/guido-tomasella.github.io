# PRODUCT.md

**Register:** brand

Personal-brand site for **Guido Tomasella — Data Manager & Tech Lead**. A single-page WebGL scroll
narrative (a particle field that morphs `dispersed → neural network → GT → "LET'S BUILD"`), not a
portfolio template and not an online CV.

## Audience
Hiring managers, CDAOs, and data/AI leaders evaluating Guido for senior Data Management / Tech Lead
roles. They are senior, time-poor, and skeptical of buzzwords.

## What it has to land
One idea, not a number wall: Guido **makes data trustworthy, then builds the agents that keep it
that way** — he amplifies governance, quality and engineering with AI and agents. Message-driven,
not metrics-driven. Nobody should leave remembering a figure; they leave remembering the idea.

## Voice
Senior, sophisticated, original, credible. Concrete over buzzwords. No marketing filler
("streamline", "empower", "world-class"), no em-dash-heavy AI cadence, no "X, not Y" rebuttal
patterns. English copy lives in `content/content.en.json`.
